QEC Meeting of Program Team(PTs) of All Department 30-03-2017

The Quality Enhancement Cell, Government College University, Faisalabad conducted meeting of Assessment Team Members (ATs) on 30-03-2017 at Sufi Barkat Hall. The purpose of the said meeting was to aware all the ATs about Self Assessment Report review process. Dr. Babak Mahmood, Director Quality Enhancement Cell shared the procedure to review Self Assessment Report The meeting was ended with concluding remarks and vote of thanks by Dr. Babak Mahmood Director QEC.

PHD & M. S/M. PHIL Program Review HEC Panel has visited the Government College University, Faisalabad for three days (OCTOBER 29 TO 31, 2018) for the purpose to review all PhD ad M. S/MPhil programs offered by the University. The Focal Person of this HEC Review was Dr. Babak Mahmood, Director, Quality Enhancement Cell, Government College University, Faisalabad. The panel was comprised of seven (07) members Prof. Dr Hafiz Muhammad Inamullah, University of Peshawar, Peshawar was the convener of this review panel, Prof. Dr Muhammad Ashraf, University of Lahore, Lahore, Prof. Dr Wasima Shahzad, Air University, Islamabad, Prof. Dr Riaz Ul Amin, BUITEMS, Quetta, Prof. Dr Saeed Ahmad Buzdar, Islamia University of Bahawalpur, Bahawalpur, Mr. Muhammad Gazanfar Bhatti, QAA, Higher Education Commission, Islamabad and Mr. Muhammad Ali Baig, QAA, Higher Education Commission, Islamabad The Panel met with Deans, Chairpersons, Registrar, Controller of Examination, Director Advance Studies, faculty members (Assistant Professors & Lecturers) and students. The panel also visited the class rooms, laboratories and libraries. At the end of the visit the panel had an exit meeting with the Worthy Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Muhammad Ali (TI) and discussed all the commendations and observations regarding the PhD & M. S/M.Phil Programs and overall facilities of the University.
